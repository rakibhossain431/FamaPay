How Fast Will My Money Get There?

Cash Pickup is available within minutes in most cases if you pay with a debit card. So are transfers to most mobile wallets and select banks. Payments via Bank Account (ACH transfer) take longer to process.

How To Send Money To Bank Accounts?

Step 1: Choose how much you want to send. Step 2: Select from the available banks in your recepients country. Step 3: Fill-out the account information of your recepient. Step 4: Pay for your transfer using FamaPay or an External account.

How To Send Money To an Agent for Cash Pickup?

Step 1: Choose how much you want to send. Step 2: Fill-out the recepients name, address, and phone number. Step 3: Select a Location for Cash Pick-up. Step 4: Pay for your transfer using FamaPay or an External account.
